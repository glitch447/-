Artist Statement

My art, a unique blend of game design, photography, painting, sketching, 3d modeling, and other mixed media, sheds light on the often misunderstood nature of mental illness. It serves as a powerful tool to educate and advocate for this topic, aiming to destigmatize mental health issues and foster understanding. Each piece is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit, inviting empathy for those with the mental illness depicted.

Each piece I create is a multi-layered narrative, inviting viewers to delve deeper and interpret their own meanings. My creative process is a delicate balance between spontaneity and deliberation, drawing inspiration from unexpected sources while maintaining a strong focus on technique. It's a journey that celebrates the resilience of the human spirit in the face of uncertainty.

My goal is not only to raise awareness but also to spark meaningful conversations and inspire action towards greater compassion and support for those affected by mental illness.
